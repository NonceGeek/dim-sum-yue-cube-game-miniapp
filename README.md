## Yue Cube · Play Your Way into Cantonese

**Yue Cube (粤玩粤醒)** is a playful, modern approach to learning Cantonese—designed for a generation that learns best through interaction, not repetition.

We turn language learning into something you *want* to come back to every day.

---

### 🌟 Our Vision
To make Cantonese learning feel natural, rewarding, and genuinely fun—  
so that practice becomes a habit, not a task.

---

### 🎮 How It Works
Instead of long lessons and heavy memorization, Yue Cube offers:

- **Game-driven learning**  
  Fill in phrases, recognize sounds, match characters—each interaction is a small step forward.

- **Daily momentum**  
  Build streaks, complete tasks, and track your progress effortlessly.

- **Lightweight experience**  
  No pressure, no overload—just quick sessions that fit into your day.
